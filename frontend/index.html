About DRONAM

DRONAM (Data for Research in Orthopedics, Navigation and Additive Manufacturing) represents India's pioneering research facility dedicated to foot and ankle biomechanics, strategically located at the prestigious PGIMER Chandigarh.

Our mission is to advance orthopedic understanding through cutting-edge research, innovative experimentation, and the integration of artificial intelligence in biomechanical analysis. We bridge the gap between traditional orthopedic research and modern AI-driven insights.
